AgriSentinel

A smart disaster alert system that monitors agricultural lands in real-time and protects farmers from forest fires and wild animal attacks — communicating in their own native language.

  • Detects real-time disasters including forest fires and wild animal attacks on farming lands
  • Sends automated voice alerts in the farmer's native language for immediate action
  • Notifies the Forest Department and Fire Department simultaneously for faster response
  • Aims to reduce crop and livestock loss in rural farming communities
